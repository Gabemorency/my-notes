# Belt of Truth 
>[!Quote]  *"Stand therefore, having girded your waist with truth"* - Ephesians 6:14

#### What does a belt do? 
A belt holds your set of armor together. There is no armour without the belt. It secures your sword to your side, secures your breastplate to your chest, and ensures that everything is where it is supposed to be. Before your armour can be put on, the belt is used to gather all the garments underneath together. The belt must be put on before anything else goes on. The belt of truth is the foundation upon which all other pieces of armour are placed upon. 

#### How does this protect us from the devil?
>[!Quote] *You are of your father the devil, and thedesires of your father you want to do. He was a murderer from the beginning, and does not stand in the truth, because there is no truth in him. When he speaks a lie, he speaks from his own resources, for he is a liar and the father of it.* - John 8:44

Satan is the father of lies. His mission is to convince us of his lies to get us to stop worshipping and following God. If He can get us to believe his lies then he does not have to worry about the other pieces of armor that we have. His lies include things such as 
- The most important thing in life is what others think about me. 
- If I do not impress my peers, I am a failure. 
- If I don't do well in school, I am dumb. 
- Jesus doesn't love me and can't forgive me. 
- Jesus is too disappointed in me. I may as well stop trying. 
- Jesus doesn't care if I disobey Him because He loves me. 
- Fear is good. 
If the devil can get us to believe his lies, he's destroyed the foundation upon which our faith is built on. The belt of truth enables us to combat the devil's lies in the same way Jesus did. With the words: *It is written.*
#### How do we equip the belt of truth? 
>[!quote] *Study to shew thyself approved unto God, a workman that needeth not to be ashamed, rightly dividing the word of truth.* - 2 Timothy 2:15

We equip ourselves with the truth by *knowing the truth.* And we know the truth by studying the Bible. When you love someone, you don't automatically know everything about them. You take time to get to know them, their likes and dislikes. How they communicate. What they believe and what they don't believe. If you don't spend time with this individual however, you'll never truly know them. We equip ourselves with the truth by knowing the truth, **Jesus Christ**, and His word.
