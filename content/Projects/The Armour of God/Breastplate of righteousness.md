# Breastplate of righteousness 
>[!Quote] *Stand therefore, having girded your waist with truth, having put on the breastplate of righteousness* - Ephesians 6:14

#### What does a breastplate do?
A breastplate has one purpose: To protect your heart and your most vital organs. 

#### How does this protect us from the devil?
Righteousness is being made right with God through accepting that our works do not achieve us salvation, but a relationship with God does. Each and every day, we must die to ourselves and accept Jesus free gift of salvation and deny ourselves fleshly desires. When we sin, our sin wounds not only our hearts, but God's as well. Obedience to God is not just so that we can be "good christians" but it also protects our hearts. God tells us not to do certain things because He knows it will hurt us. 

#### How do we equip the breastplate of righteousness
We equip the breastplate of righteousness by listening to and following God. Righteousness is closely associated with blamelessness which is when no one can find fault against you. We should strive to be both blameless and righteousness in the eyes of God. We must protect our hearts.
