## Decisions Made

> [!note] Decision 1 — Eastside expansion timeline
> **Resolved:** Move forward with phased Eastside expansion beginning Q1 2025. Phase 1 scoped to two pilot sites. Full rollout contingent on Harlow Foundation renewal.
>
> **Rationale:** Demand data from the community survey supports the need. Phased approach limits financial exposure if funding doesn't materialize.
>
> **Owner:** Lucía Fernández
> **Deadline:** Pilot site selection by November 1

> [!note] Decision 2 — Communications role
> **Resolved:** Hire a part-time contractor (20 hrs/week) rather than a full-time staff position for the communications role. Revisit in Q2 2025 based on budget performance.
>
> **Rationale:** Cash flow uncertainty in H1 2025 makes a full-time commitment risky. Contractor arrangement gives flexibility.
>
> **Owner:** Renata Osei
> **Deadline:** Post listing by September 30
