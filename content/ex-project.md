## Objective

Launch two pilot program sites in the Eastside corridor by end of Q1 2025. Validate the expansion model before committing to full rollout.

**Success looks like:** Both sites operational, baseline community engagement data collected, and a clear recommendation on whether full rollout is viable.

## Notes and Context

**Why this project exists:** Community demand data from the 2024 survey showed strong support for Eastside expansion. The Eastside Community Alliance partnership is time-sensitive — delaying risked losing both the partnership and community momentum.

**What makes this complicated:** Two board members dissented on financial risk grounds. Their concerns were valid and are documented. This project is a calculated risk, not a dismissal of those concerns. The phased structure was designed specifically to limit exposure.

**What we're watching:** The Harlow renewal is the load-bearing assumption. Everything downstream of that is contingent.
