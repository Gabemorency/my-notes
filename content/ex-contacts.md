# Contact Manager

This note is a structured index of key relationships for The Commons Initiative. Each entry captures not just contact information but *context* — how we know this person, what matters in our relationship with them, and what we've learned over time.

> [!tip] Why this exists
> When staff turn over, contact lists survive. Context doesn't. This system is designed to make the relationship — not just the name and email — transferable.

## Funders

**Primary contact:** Sandra Okafor, Program Officer — Community Infrastructure
**Email:** s.okafor@harlowfoundation.org
**Phone:** Available on request (relationship-based — do not cold-call)

**Relationship history:**
- First contact: March 2021 via introduction from Board member Yolanda Reyes
- First grant: $45,000, 2022 cycle — neighborhood safety programming
- Current grant: $78,000, 2023–2024 — Eastside pilot funding pending renewal

**What we know:**
Sandra responds best to data-led communications. She has flagged twice that she wants to see "systems thinking, not just program delivery" in our reporting. She is championing a new internal priority around organizational resilience — our knowledge infrastructure work directly aligns with this.

She is not the final decision-maker. The grants committee meets in November. Relationships with committee members are underdeveloped — worth building before next cycle.

**Last contact:** September 14, 2024 (Q3 strategy meeting update via Dayo)
**Next touchpoint:** October 1 (follow-up on written renewal confirmation)

## Partners

### Eastside Community Alliance

**Primary contact:** Mireille Johansson, Executive Director
**Email:** mireille@eastsidealliance.org

**Relationship context:**
Key implementation partner for the Eastside pilot. Mireille has been collaborative and candid about what hasn't worked in past partnerships with external organizations. She specifically flagged "not being kept in the loop on decisions that affect our community" as a past failure point. We have committed to shared decision logs for the pilot.

**What we know:**
Mireille has significant credibility in the Eastside corridor. Her endorsement opens doors. Her skepticism closes them. Relationship management here is high-stakes.

**Last contact:** August 30, 2024
**Next touchpoint:** November 1 (pilot site assessment coordination)
