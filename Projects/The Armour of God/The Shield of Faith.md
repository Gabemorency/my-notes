# The Shield of Faith 
>[!Quote] *above all, taking the shield of faith with which you will be able to quench all the fiery darts of the wicked one.* - Ephesians 6:16

#### What does a shield do?
When Paul wrote this passage, Roman soldiers carried shields that were covered with heavy animal hide. Before a battle, they would dip their shields into the water so that when fiery darts hit them, the wet hide would extinguish the darts. A shield protects you from things that your armour can not, which in battle, happens to be most weapons. Even further more, a shield ensures that the enemy cannot tough you with his/her weapon. 

#### How does this protect us from the devil?
The verse says *"Above all, taking the shield of faith..."* There is an emphasis placed on the shield that is not placed on the other weapons. Faith might be one of the most important aspects of our relationship with God. Our entire belief system is built on faith. When you say it out loud, what we believe sounds quite strange. We believe that a man named Jesus spoke and the world came into existence. His solution for the worlds evil was to come back to earth in the form of a baby to a virgin, live, perform miracles, and die. After that He was raised from the dead and returned to heaven. Pretty crazy stuff... When you think about it though, our faith is the only thing that stands between us and disbelief. Our faith is what keeps us going when things get impossibly hard. Our faith is what gives up hope, even when we don't understand. 

#### How do we equip the shield of faith? 
>[!Quote] *Faith comes by hearing, and hearing by the word of God.* - Romans 10:7

We can increase our faith by studying our history (The bible), and by asking God to increase our faith. We equip the shield of faith when we know what we believe, and when we can trust in it enough to risk everything for it.
