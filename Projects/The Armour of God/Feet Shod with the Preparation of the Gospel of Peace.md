# Feet Shod with the Preparation of the Gospel of Peace 
>[!Quote] *and having shod your feet with the preparation of the gospel of peace* - Ephesians 6:15

#### What does footwear in war do?
Footwear in war is incredibly important. If you cannot walk, or are walking on a weak foundation, the enemy will destroy you. **Preparation** is a word meaning: **A prepared foundation**. The gospel provides the footing for everything we do. You can have the strongest shield, and be the best swordsman, but if your footing is weak, none of that matters. 

#### How does this protect us from the devil?
If footwear is the most important part of the armour, the devil will try to take away your footing. He will do this through presenting to you messages and gospels that are convincing and *sound good* But are not the gospel of peace and of God. Have you ever heard the saying: 
- Follow your heart? - ***Jeremiah 17:9-10** “The heart is deceitful above all things, And desperately wicked; Who can know it?*
- Believe in yourself? - ***John 14:6*** *Believe in me "I am the way, the truth, and the life."*
- Live your truth - ***John 8:32*** *"And you shall know the truth, and the truth shall set you free."*
- Your happiness is all that matters. - ***Mark 8:36-37*** *For what will it profit a man if he gains the whole world, and loses his own soul?*
There is only one gospel, and that gospel is the foundation upon all of our beliefs. The devil will try and create commandments, ideas, and doctrines in the bible for us to follow and ensnare ourselves in. Just look at what he did with the pharisees. As a result of the devil, they literally missed Jesus when we has standing right in front of them. 

#### How do we equip this footwear? 
We equip this footwear by being prepared with the gospel. Not only to fight against the devil but to minister to others. Church is not the only time or place that ministry is supposed to happen. We're supposed to be evangelizing the entire world everywhere we go, and in whatever we do. Being prepared with the gospel ensures that we can speak life and truth into ourselves, and it also gives us the ability to go forth, and give it to others. We are not to keep it to ourselves, we are to give it to others.
