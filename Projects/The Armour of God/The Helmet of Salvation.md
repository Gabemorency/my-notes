# The Helmet of Salvation 
>[!Quote] *"And take the helmet of salvation"* - Ephesians 6:17

#### What does a helmet do?
A helmet is the most important part of armor in a soldiers kit. Your head is critical. Soldiers do not go into battle without a helmet. 

#### How does this protect us from the devil?
>[!Quote] *"Do not conform to the pattern of this world but be transformed by the renewing of your mind. Then you will be able to test and approve what God’s will is—his good, pleasing, and perfect will.”* - Romans 12:2

Every action, every deed that we do, everything in our lives, starts with a thought. Many christians never get where they're trying to go, because while they're fighting on Jesus' side, their thoughts are living in enemy territory. Salvation is our hope and faith that God will deliver us and has a plan for us as long as we follow Him and trust in Him. Are you guarding your thoughts? Everything we do, we should do in the mind of Jesus. Would you want Him listening to that music that you listen to? Would you want Him watching whatever it is that you watch? Would you want Him liking what you post or going through your phone? Our salvation begins with our thoughts, and if our thoughts are not taken captive unto God, how can we expect Him to transform us. If we don't surrender to Him, how can we expect Him to transform us? 

#### How do you equip the helmet of salvation?
>[!Quote] *Let this mind be in you which was also in Christ Jesus* - Philippians 2:5-11

We equip the helmet of salvation by allowing God to transform our minds into a mind of His. When we guard our hearts and our minds against worldly things, and fill our minds with things that are of God, we equip the helm of salvation. We cannot give ourselves partly to God. Salvation is received when we surrender **Completely** to God. Whatever we withhold from God, we give to the devil. Often times, we end up fighting a losing battle in our walk with Christ simple because we withhold things from God. 

- if you don’t fill your mind with Christ, you fill it with anxiety, doubt, sin, and fear 
- The devil doesn’t have to make you bad, he just has to make you busy
