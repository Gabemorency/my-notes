# The Sword of the Spirit 
>[!Quote] *and the sword of the Spirit, which is the word of God* - Ephesians 6:16

#### What does a sword do? 
A sword is unique in the sense that it is the only weapon that is both defensive and offensive. It is also the only part of the armour of God thats ability depends directly on the skill of the user. If you don't know how to use a sword, it will be useless. 

#### How does this protect you from the devil? 
To know how this protects us from the devil, we need to know what the sword of the spirit is. The sword of the spirit is the *word of God.* It's important to understand that the only thing that can defeat the devil is God's word. We cant resist him alone, we cannot overpower him, we cant wrestle with him. We are too weak in our own strength. It is only the word of God that can overcome the devil. 

#### How do you equip the sword of the spirit?
You equip the sword of the spirit when you know God's word. If you don't know God's word, you cannot fight. I had a unique experience with my friend in Tennessee where a street preacher came and told us that we were going to hell. She told us that the mark of the beast was the vaccine and that Bill Gates was the antichrist. Now what was most interesting was that this lady was using scripture to back up her claims. She was using scripture to tell us that the sabbath was a pagan holiday. She was using scripture to tell us that unless we did what she and her religion commanded, we would be going to hell. Now if I'm being honest, If we didn't know scripture and what it says about salvation, the sabbath, and Jesus, we would've been swayed. But our knowledge of the scripture enabled us to stand firm and "fight" the lies that this woman was sharing. Often times, the devil will use the bible to try and sift us. The bible was used to justify slavery, has been used to justify sexism, has been used to oppress entire races of people. If we do not know the scripture, we cannot fight back against the lies of satan.
