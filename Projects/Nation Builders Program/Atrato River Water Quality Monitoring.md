# Atrato River Water Quality Monitoring
*Aurielle*
**Purpose**:  
Monitors the Atrato River's pH, turbidity, and temperature to protect biodiversity and improve community health.

### AI Integrations:
- **Real-Time Analysis**: Categorizes water quality (e.g., excellent, dangerous).  
- **Trend Detection**: Identifies contaminant changes over time.  
- **Predictive Monitoring**: Issues real-time alerts for water quality changes.
